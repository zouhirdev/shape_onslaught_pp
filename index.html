Privacy Policy for Shape Onslaught

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Shape Onslaught ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application.

By using Shape Onslaught, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect specific data to ensure the game functions correctly, to save your progress, and to provide advertising services. This includes:

  • Device Identifiers: We may collect Device IDs, Advertising IDs (such as GAID), and other unique identifiers to manage guest accounts and serve personalized ads.
  • Game Progress & Activity: Data regarding your level, XP, inventory, currency (Shards), and unlocked items is stored in the cloud to allow for save retrieval.
  • Purchase History: We store records of In-App Purchases to allow for content unlocking and purchase restoration.
  • Approximate Location: Used solely by our advertising partners to serve relevant ads.
  • Crash & Performance Data: Technical data used to identify bugs and improve game stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To authenticate players, save progress to the cloud, and maintain leaderboards.
  • In-App Purchases: To process transactions via Google Play Billing.
  • Advertising: To display banner and rewarded video ads via Google AdMob.
  • Analytics: To understand how players interact with the game and fix technical issues.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active to ensure you do not lose your progress.

Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data (including progress, inventory, and leaderboard scores).

To request deletion, please email us at the contact address below with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include your Player ID (found in the game settings or main menu) to facilitate this process.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted via SSL (HTTPS). However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and reliable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
