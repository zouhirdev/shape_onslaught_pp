Request Account Deletion

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data for the mobile application Shape Onslaught.

How to Request Deletion

Because our game relies on third-party backend services (LootLocker) and does not have a dedicated web login portal, account deletion is handled via a manual support request. Please follow these steps:

Step 1: Locate your Identity

If you still have the app installed, open Shape Onslaught, go to the Main Menu, and note your Player ID (displayed near the settings or version number).

If you have uninstalled the app, please provide the email address associated with your Google Play Games account.

Step 2: Send Request

Click the button below to generate an email request, or send an email manually.

Send Deletion Request Email

Or email us at: zouhirdev@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently removed from our active databases:

  • Account Profile: Your unique player ID and link to Google Play Games Services.
  • Game Progress: Level, XP, Shards, unlocked upgrades, and campaign progress.
  • Inventory: All virtual items and ship upgrades acquired.
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your high scores will be anonymized or removed from global leaderboards.

Data Retention

Please note that we may retain certain data for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction Records: History of In-App Purchases (via Google Play) may be retained for financial reporting and tax purposes. This data is not linked to your game profile after deletion.
  • Backup Archives: Data may persist in secure backups for a short period (up to 30 days) until those backups are overwritten.

Response Time

We aim to process all deletion requests within 14 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
